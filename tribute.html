Smt. Lata Mangeshkar

Indian playback singer

Here's a time line of Smt. Lata Mangeshkar's life:


  • 1929 - Born in Indore, Madhya Pradesh.
  • 1942 - She started her singing career by singing for a Marathi film.
  • 1945 - Moved to Mumbai.
  • 1948 - Master Vinayak, her early mentor passed away.
  • 1950 - Lata had become a popular playback singer in Bollywood.
  • 1969 - She won the Padma Bhushan
  • 1993 - Bestowed with Life time achievement award
  • 1999 - She was honored with the Padma Vibhushan
  • 2001 - Winner of the Bharat Ratna award .
  • 2009 - Honoured with France's highhest order, the title of officer of the French Legion of Honour.
  • 2012 - Music label LM Music came to life, with an album of bhajans that she sang with her sister Usha.
  • 2015 - Mangeshkar's last song 'Jeena Hai Kya' for a film.
  • 2022 - India lost her Nightingale. Her age was 92yrs.
"The kind and caring Lata Didi has left us. She leaves a void in our nation that cannot be filled. The coming generations will remember her as a stalwart of Indian culture, whose melodious voice had an unparalleled ability to mesmerise people."
